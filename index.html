Our Third Anniversary

by SWAPNANIL DUTTAmay 8th, 2020

Everything started on 8th May 2017 when you finally confessed your love for me.

Sreyoshi,

These 3 years have gone like a roller-coaster ride. May be things didn’t start like a fairy tale romance for us but I’m going to make sure our future is. I have never written a love letter for you, maybe I just can’t but that doesn’t mean I don’t love you. Each day I things turn out to be great for us. We misunderstand, we argue, we blame but at the end of the day we are and will be together.

I am not so good at writing love letters but this love letter
has more memories than any love letter would have ever made. We have spent so
much time together, although you staying away from me, you’re always close and
picking me up wherever I fall. Our memories together are too much for any story
to say so here are some of the pictures which may relive those moments for us.
